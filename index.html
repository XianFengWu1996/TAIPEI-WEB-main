Best chinese food in — Quincy

Hello, we are Taipei Cuisine, located on Billings Road. We offer some of the best Chinese and Taiwanese style food in the area.

Delivery

Delivery wait times are usually 45 minutes. Time varies during peak hours & bad weathers, please allow more time in these situations. Delivery fees are base on distance and starts from $ 2 and covers about two miles. Each addition mile will cost a $ 1 more. Tips are not included.

Pick-Up

The pick-up wait time are usually 15 minutes. Time varies during peak hours, please allow more time during peak hours. All the dumplings and buns are house-made and steam upon request. The time for steaming are around 10-15 minutes. Tips are not require, but are appreciated

Taiwanese & Sichuanese

Offering the best of both worlds. The dishes are created by chefs who are high experienced with the each of the styles. The Sichuanese food are well-known to be spicy and is marked with a red pepper with red texts on the menu.

Parking

Many parking spaces are available on Billings Road. The parking space on Pierce Street and Felton Street are also available. There is a public parking lot right across the resturant in Vane Street. Most of the time limit for the parking are 90 minutes.
